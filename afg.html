What are the Documents Required for an Afghanistan Visa Application?

Requirements for an Afghanistan visa depend on the type of visa you are applying for. For example, a tourist visa requires a passport valid for at least six months beyond your planned stay in Afghanistan, while a business visa requires an invitation letter from a company or organization in Afghanistan.

The required documents vary depending on the type of visa you are applying for, so it is important to consult the specific embassy or consulate website.

Some general requirements for all visas include a valid passport, two recent passport-style photos, and proof of travel insurance. Additionally, some visas (e.g. tourist) may require documentation of your finances in case you are refused entry to Afghanistan. Most embassies and consulates will not issue visas unless all required documents have been submitted. Please be sure to check with the embassy or consulate in your country before beginning the application process.
